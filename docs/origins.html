Origins

Technically, Origins are sets of abilities that generally follow a mob-based theme. Canonically, Origins are humanoid races with many different backgrounds and cultures that may still live on in the different Minecraft dimensions. There are 13 different Origins that come premade in this datapack, but most abilities are structured so that players can create their own Origins, too. See Add-Ons for more details.
