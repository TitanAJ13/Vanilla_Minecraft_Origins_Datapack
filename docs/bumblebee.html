Bumblebee

Bumblebees are happy and buzzy, but they can cause problems when angry. Just like bees and cave spiders, they inflict poison upon attacks. The only difference is these Bumblebees can hold weapons, too! Their wings are not strong enough to fly like bees do, but they can still float around unless it's raining. Bumblebees can't move around as easily with wet wings, so they should stay out of the rain.
