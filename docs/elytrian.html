Elytrian

Elytrians are part of an ancient race that was nearly wiped out long ago, and the remnants of their civilization can still be found in End Cities and Ships. They have kept their wings and armor-crafting practices to ensure they can still fly while protected. However, they are frail due to age and must be careful not to have any sudden stops.
