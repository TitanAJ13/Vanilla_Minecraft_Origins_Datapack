Advancements

Advancements are achievements or goals to pursue in the game. They often include obtaining certain items to progress, or accessing new biomes, structures, etc. The Vanilla Origins Datapack includes a new set of Advancements that are related to the many abilities and new items available. Each of these is listed below in categories.
